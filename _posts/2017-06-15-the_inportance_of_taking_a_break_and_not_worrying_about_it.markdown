title:  "The inportance of taking a break (and not worrying about it)"
date:   2017-06-15 15:41:53 +0000
---

When I just started the course, I was anxious to move along and finish the program as fast as possible. However, I took me longer than I had expected to complete the final CLI Data Gem Project. As I got stuck and didn't know how to move further, I made an appointment with the curator. Her schedule was booked, so I had to wait for almost a week to discuss my project with her. Then, I was only able to schedule a review one week from the day I felt like it was ready. This whole time I was impatient and anxious, felt like I was not doing enough. When I finally had my project reviewed, I was ready to go on a vacation I had planned in advance. 

At first, I thought that I would work whenever I could, but it was only stressing me out more. So I have decided to take a break and refused to feel guilty about it (which, honestly, took a while to work on). Now, I am back in New York with a fresh mind and refilled energy batteries and ready to start a new chapter!
